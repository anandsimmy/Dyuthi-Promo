Who We Are

About Us

This time around, Dyuthi is going to be celebrated as a National Level Arts Fest. Dyuthi is always the biggest carnival in GEC, where a blend of the most talented and the most diverse array of energetic college going youth from the South of India gather. Unlike previous years in which the artistic creativity of its participants had to be complimented by technical acumen, this year Dyuthi allows its participants to let flow their creative energy. Just like its meaning, Dyuthi is a festival that glows, bringing together the zeal and zest of everyone involved to make something truly special and memorable.
